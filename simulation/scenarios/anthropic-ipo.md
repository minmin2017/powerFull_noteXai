# Scenario: Anthropic IPO — Buy or Sell?

Anthropic has announced plans for an IPO in the coming months. The AI lab behind Claude is reportedly seeking a valuation of $50–80 billion. 

Key facts:
- Anthropic raised $7.3B from Google and Amazon; both are strategic investors with board influence
- Revenue growing rapidly but company is not yet profitable (heavy compute costs)
- Strong enterprise adoption of Claude API; Claude Code is gaining developer traction
- Key risk: OpenAI, Google DeepMind, and Meta all competing aggressively
- Regulatory scrutiny of AI is increasing globally (EU AI Act, US executive orders)
- Lock-up period: early employees/investors cannot sell for 6 months post-IPO
- Dario Amodei (CEO) has emphasized safety over speed — which some investors see as a risk

The question: **Should retail investors buy Anthropic stock at IPO, wait, or avoid?**

Simulate a panel of experts debating this over multiple rounds as new information emerges.
