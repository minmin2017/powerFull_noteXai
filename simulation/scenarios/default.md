# Scenario: City Hall Corruption Leak

A whistleblower has anonymously leaked documents suggesting that the city mayor secretly approved a controversial $200M stadium construction contract in exchange for campaign donations from MegaBuild Corp.

Key facts at the start:
- Documents are unverified but spreading fast on social media
- Mayor's office has issued a brief "no comment"
- MegaBuild Corp stock dropped 12% this morning
- The stadium was supposed to replace a low-income housing district
- Local elections are in 3 months

The simulation will track how each character reacts as the story develops over multiple rounds.
