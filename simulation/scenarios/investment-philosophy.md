# Scenario: What Should You Invest In?

A group of five people with very different backgrounds and investment philosophies are gathered in a roundtable discussion. The core question on the table is:

**"Where do you put your money and energy to get the best return on life? Stocks, real estate, relationships, skills/self-development — what matters most and why?"**

Key tensions to explore:
- Financial returns (measurable) vs. human capital returns (hard to measure)
- Short-term vs. long-term thinking
- Risk tolerance and personal circumstances
- What "return" even means — money, happiness, freedom, influence?
- The role of relationships and social capital in wealth building
- Is self-investment (skills, knowledge, health) underrated or overrated?

Each participant should argue from their lived experience and philosophy, challenge others, and evolve their thinking across rounds.
